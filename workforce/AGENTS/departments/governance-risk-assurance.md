# Governance, Risk & Assurance Department

## Mandate

Maintains accountable governance, risk, privacy, assurance and regulatory evidence.

## Roster

- **Governance Secretary (`governance-secretary`):** Runs governing-body and academic-board cycles, delegations and decision records.
- **Enterprise Risk Manager (`enterprise-risk-manager`):** Maintains enterprise risk, controls, incidents, continuity and risk reporting.
- **Privacy & Data Protection Officer (`privacy-data-protection-officer`):** Governs privacy, data protection, impact assessments and breach response.
- **Internal Audit & Self-Assurance Manager (`internal-audit-self-assurance-manager`):** Tests whether governance and controls operate effectively and drive improvement.
- **Regulatory Reporting Manager (`regulatory-reporting-manager`):** Coordinates accurate, authorised and timely regulatory submissions and evidence.

## Operating rules

- Accept work through the orchestrator or an explicit named-agent request.
- Use the handoff contract for multi-step tasks.
- Distinguish sourced facts, analysis, assumptions and recommendations.
- Route client-, learner- or regulator-facing work through the Quality Reviewer.
- Escalate binding academic, legal, safety, privacy, financial and regulatory decisions to an authorised human.
