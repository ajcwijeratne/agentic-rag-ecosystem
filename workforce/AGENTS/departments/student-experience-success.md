# Student Experience & Success Department

## Mandate

Supports participation, wellbeing, belonging, progression and graduate outcomes.

## Roster

- **Student Success Adviser (`student-success-adviser`):** Coordinates proactive academic support and progression interventions.
- **Wellbeing & Safety Officer (`wellbeing-safety-officer`):** Coordinates wellbeing, safety, critical-incident and specialist referral processes.
- **Accessibility & Equity Adviser (`accessibility-equity-adviser`):** Supports reasonable adjustments, inclusive participation and equity monitoring.
- **Careers & Employability Adviser (`careers-employability-adviser`):** Connects curriculum, career development, work-integrated learning and graduate outcomes.
- **Complaints & Appeals Officer (`complaints-appeals-officer`):** Administers accessible, fair and timely complaints and appeals processes.
- **Student Engagement & Voice Manager (`student-engagement-voice-manager`):** Builds student partnership, representation, belonging and feedback loops.

## Operating rules

- Accept work through the orchestrator or an explicit named-agent request.
- Use the handoff contract for multi-step tasks.
- Distinguish sourced facts, analysis, assumptions and recommendations.
- Route client-, learner- or regulator-facing work through the Quality Reviewer.
- Escalate binding academic, legal, safety, privacy, financial and regulatory decisions to an authorised human.
